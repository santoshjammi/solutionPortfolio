About Us

Incorporated on 13 Dec 2011, Metkan Engineering & Services (P) Limited ("MESPL or Metkan") was earlier a group company of Boyance Infrastructure Private Limited, one of the fastest growing companies in the domain of Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC), development of residential and commercial spaces, Hospitality Services, Engineering Services and NBFC activities over the last many years. There are two branches of Metkan Engineering & Services (P) Limited across Visakhapatnam.

Its continuous focus on innovation and expansion together with its commitment to quality and excellence contributed significantly to the progress of the company. We've expanded our portfolio to include cutting-edge IT and Digital services in Ameya Labs which is incubated as our sister concern.

We aim to excel in engineering and digital contracts, enlarging our capability continuously in the value chain from concept to commissioning. We achieve this though commitment to quality, safety, environment, cost economics and delivery.

Our Leadership

Mr. K.V. Srikanth

Chief Executive Officer (CEO) & Director, Metkan Engineering & Services

Srikanth Kotih, CEO of METKAN Engineering & Services, is a seasoned mechanical engineer with over 24 years of professional experience spanning marine, manufacturing, fabrication, and infrastructure sectors. He has successfully led complex EPC projects, including large-scale earthworks for railways and roads, coal plant assemblies, and electrical transmission line erection. Under his visionary leadership, METKAN has excelled in project planning, onsite fabrication, installation, testing, and commissioning of both mechanical and electrical systems. Srikanth is recognized for building high-performing, value-driven teams, fostering innovation, and consistently delivering client-centric solutions that meet industry standards for quality, safety, and reliability.

Mr. Santosh Jammi

Chief Technology Officer (CTO), Metkan Engineering & Services

Santosh Jammi is a versatile software engineer recognized for solving complex technical problems across the enterprise software lifecycle. With 16+ years of experience, he excels in automating processes, reducing regression cycles from thousands to hundreds of hours, and improving application reliability for legacy systems. Santosh has led DevOps teams, introduced CI/CD pipelines, and delivered infrastructure automation projects that enhance productivity and performance. His approach centers on clear problem definition, practical technology application, and learning-driven solution design. Santosh’s track record includes client issue resolution, technical leadership, and driving impactful change through thoughtful engineering practices .
