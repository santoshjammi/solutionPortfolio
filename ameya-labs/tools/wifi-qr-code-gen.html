How Wi-Fi QR Codes Work

A Wi-Fi QR code makes connecting to any network simple and fast. Instead of manually typing in a long and complex password, people can use their smartphone's camera to scan the code. This automatically populates the network name (SSID), encryption type, and password, streamlining the connection process. It's a perfect solution for homes, businesses, or public spaces.
