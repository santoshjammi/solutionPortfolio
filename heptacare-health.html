Phase 1: Must-Haves vs. Good-to-Haves

A strategic approach to deliver immediate value while planning for future growth.

Must-Haves for Immediate Launch

  • User & Staff Registration: Onboarding via CRM-integrated SSO.
  • Patient Lead Management: Create, manage, and track patient records.
  • Appointment Booking: Multi-party confirmation workflow for bookings.
  • Post-Appointment Tracking: Status updates for every lead from a single dashboard.

Good-to-Haves for Later Phases

  • Advanced MFA: WhatsApp, Signal, and Telegram integration.
  • Telemedicine & Virtual Care: Video calls, AI translation, and e-prescriptions.
  • OCR & AI Analytics: Document autofill and predictive reports.
  • Insurance Pre-Authorization: Full API-driven workflow.

Navigating Key Risks for National Growth

A proactive approach to ensuring long-term success and scalability.

Regulatory & Compliance

Expanding across India means navigating complex, state-specific healthcare and data privacy laws. Non-compliance is a major risk.

Built-in Compliance: The foundation is built on major laws like HIPAA, GDPR, and DPDP/India.

Granular RBAC: Access is strictly limited by role and location, minimizing exposure.

Comprehensive Audit Trails: All actions on patient data are logged for accountability.

Technical Scalability & Performance

A massive increase in users and data volume could degrade system performance, leading to a poor user experience.

Scalable Cloud Infrastructure: Hosted on a robust platform to handle exponential growth.

Real-time Data Sync: The architecture is built for speed to prevent bottlenecks during high-volume operations.
