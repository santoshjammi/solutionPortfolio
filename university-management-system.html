Streamlining Operations and Enhancing the Academic Experience

1. Introduction

This document proposes the development and implementation of a University Management System (UMS) for the university's Business Administration program. The UMS is an integrated platform designed to digitize and streamline key administrative, academic, and operational processes.

By centralizing data and automating workflows, the system will enhance efficiency, improve communication, and provide valuable insights for decision-making across all university stakeholders.
